More Carbon Emission Reduction Tips

Using Geothermal Energy

Switching to geothermal energy, which is a clean, renewable energy source generated and stored withn the earth, can save a person up to 70 percent in energy costs, or about $200 off of a $300 electric bill. Geothermal power uses the consistent temperature below the earth's surface to heat and cool homes in a more efficient, affordable and longer-lasting way than tradiional HVAC systems.

Water Consumption Tips
  1. Use low-flush toilets
  2. Use water and energy efficient washing machines
  3. Use filtered tap water for drinking rather than energy and water-intensive bottled water
  4. Take a shower rather than a bath and shower less often in less time!
  5. Turn off the taps when brushing your teeth or washing or shaving
  6. Clean your clothes less often and only wash full loads
  7. Use 'grey' water to water your garden and house plants

Thermostat Tips

Home heating and cooling counts for an average of 17 percent of carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions annually. Air leaks around doors, windows and in walls account for between 15 to 25 percent on heating and cooling energy losses in a home. Consider getting a programmable thermostat and using it properly, and sealing up air leaks around doors, windows and in walls.
